The Island

Taniti is a small tropical island (< 500 sq. miles) with sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforest, and a mountainous interior featuring a small, active volcano. About 20,000 people live here; fishing and agriculture were dominant until recent tourism growth.

Taniti City

Native architecture, bayfront beaches, museums, galleries, and nightlife. Easy to explore on foot.

Merriton Landing

Rapidly developing area on Yellow Leaf Bay with pubs (including a microbrewery), dance club, arcade, bowling, galleries, and tour operators.

Visitor Services

One hospital and several clinics; many multilingual staff. English widely spoken by younger residents.

Groceries & Shopping

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one 24-hour convenience store — handy for snacks, beach supplies, and family needs.

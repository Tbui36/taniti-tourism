Where to Eat

Taniti has 10 restaurants: five focus on local fish & rice, three serve American-style meals, and two offer Pan-Asian cuisine.

Local Cuisine

Fresh seafood and island staples—don’t miss daily catch specials.

American-Style

Comfortable choices for families and picky eaters.

Pan-Asian

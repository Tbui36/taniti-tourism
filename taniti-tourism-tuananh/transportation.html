Getting to & Around Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air. A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay one night per week. The airport currently serves small jets and propeller planes, with expansion planned to accommodate larger jets.

Public Buses

Serve Taniti City daily, 5 a.m.–11 p.m. Private buses cover the rest of the island.

Taxis & Car Rentals

Taxis operate in Taniti City; a local rental agency near the airport provides cars. Helmets required by law for bikes.

Walk & Bike

Taniti City is flat and very walkable. Multiple vendors rent bikes and helmets.

Top Activities

Most visitors come for beaches, rainforest exploration, and the volcano—plus tours by boat, bus, or helicopter.

Beaches

Swim and sun around Yellow Leaf Bay’s white sands.

Rainforest Hikes

Guided trails with rich flora and fauna; zip-lining available.

Volcano Visit

Safe, guided visits to Taniti’s active volcano (inland).

Fishing & Snorkeling

Chartered fishing tours and excellent snorkeling spots.

Museums & Culture

Local history museum, galleries, and cultural sites.

Nightlife & Fun

Pubs, microbrewery, dance club, arcade, bowling, and a cinema.
